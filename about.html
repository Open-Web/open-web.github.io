About

Open Web is a student organization at Boston University that was founded in 2014 with the goal to bring active students together to work on apps and other tech projects.

Originally our members worked with nonprofits and made websites for them. But starting from the Fall 2015 we decided to change the direction. Open Web now is an app incubator - a closed organization where students in small teams work on their own app ideas. You can read more about it here.

To join our team, you'll need to fill out an application. Even if you don't apply, we organize plenty of workshops and events open to the entire BU community. Make sure that you're subscribed to our mailing list, so you don't miss any updates.

You can also find us on Facebook, Twitter, Medium, and Instagram. If you have any questions, you can email us at openweb@bu.edu.
